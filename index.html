MULTI/TIMER

About

This is a small project I made after being frustrated with cooking timer websites. I don't have a mechanical one, and I often have to track the timing of multiple things, which none of these websites do particularly well. Instead of opening multiple timer pages and trying to remember what each was for, I made this instead!
